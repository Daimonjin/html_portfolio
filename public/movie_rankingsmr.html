I Pity The Fool Presents

Top 3 Lovecraftian Movies

by Daimonjin

1. In The Mouth of Madnesss

It stars Sam Neil as a insurance detective tasked with investigating the dissappearance of horror icon, Sutter Cane.

2. Event Horizon

It stars Lawrence Fishburne and Sam Neil as part of a crew tasked with investigatin the reappearance of a shipcalled the Event Horizon which mysteriously disappeared during a test of its gravity drive.

3. Underwater

It stars Kristen Stewart as a member of a drilling crew that encounters unknown horrors released after an earthquake destroys their facility.
